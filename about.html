Biography


My name is Haifeng Jin. I am interested in competitive programming. During my undergraduate life, I founded ACM-ICPC Club in Beijing Forestry University, where I had a great time with a bunch of awesome people with the same interest.

Then I went to Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunication for my Master's degree, where I did some research on Data Mining and Security.

Driven by the deep passion to become a computer scientist, I decided to go to United States to continue my professional development. Now, I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Texas A&M University. I work in DATA Lab with Dr. Xia Hu. My research area is Data Mining. You can find me on Google Scholar.
