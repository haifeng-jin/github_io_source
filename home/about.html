Biography


My name is Haifeng Jin. I am interested in competitive programming. During my undergraduate life, I founded ACM-ICPC Club in Beijing Forestry University, where I had a great time with a bunch of awesome people with the same interest.

Driven by the deep passion to become a computer scientist, I decided to go to United States to continue my professional development. Now, I am a PhD student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University.

My research area is Internet of Things and Edge Computing. You can find me on Google Scholar. Still a long way to go.
